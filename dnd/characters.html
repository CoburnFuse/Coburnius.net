I have played various characters, both in (short) campaigns, as well as one-shots. Not all of them have been played until the end though due to various reasons. Some characters are created with homebrew rules in mind that were used during those campaigns or one-shots. Some also have items that were created by the DMs. So keep this in mind when looking through the character sheets. The character portraits are created in Heroforge and the character sheets that I used are created by Flapkan.

Some of these characters are based on fictional characters, while others are an original concept. Keep in mind that not everything of the sheet is filled in, especially when it comes to backstory. While most of them do actually have a backstory and such, I have a very hard time writing it down properly. I may come back to them at some point to write down some more detailed character info at some point.
