“You don't get it, I'm gonna race in the Redline someday! I've busted my ass every day practicing for it, I don't have time to make stupid mistakes like this! I'm not like you amateurs, you're all talk and no action. All you losers do is sit and watch instead of getting out here with the rest of us!”

Originally, I found out about this movie because a friend recommended that I watch it, mainly praising its visuals, and he sure wasn't wrong about that. It's very clear there was a lot of love put into this movie, which shows. While it was not a box office hit, it doesn't take away how awesome this movie is. After all, it has a cult following for a very good reason.

I will immediately put it out here that the story itself is nothing special, it's a somewhat typical race story. However, it's the characters that make it incredibly interesting. The personalities of the characters, as well as the dynamic between the characters feel natural and real. Some examples being, JP who is an aloof and chill racer who is just out there to have fun and win, and Frisbee who, despite all the shadiness and pride, genuinely cares for JP.

Now, the visuals of the movie, where do I even start with this. Describing it as awesome and beautiful would genuinely just be understating it. Everything is so vibrant and fluid (and also fittingly goofy), the designs of the characters feel right and despite there being so many different designs, none of them feel out of place. Watching it on a monitor or a TV does not do the visuals justice. However, it's not just the visuals that are great, the music and audio itself is also fantastic. The music fits the entire style of the visuals completely, and (un)fortunately the music during the Yellow Line race is living rent free in my head because it is such a banger of a track.

I have stated already that the story in itself is nothing noteworthy, but that's not a reason to not watch this movie. Whether you watch it in English or Japanese (or another language,) the visuals alone should be enough to keep you excited at all times!
