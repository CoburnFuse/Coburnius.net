“Don't forget your purpose. You exist to eradicate demons from the world.”

I found out about this game a long time before it was released while scrolling through the game news site I always visit. The idea of a hack and slash with bullet hell elements very much intrigued me so I was making sure to keep myself up to date with news regarding the game. When the demo was out, it was a bit rough around the edges (for a beta demo mind you!), but overall I really enjoyed it.

So when the full game came out, I immediately purchased it, sat down, and played it and boy, did it deliver. The graphics look fantastic, the combat feels fluid and very satisfying, especially when you need to parry multiple attacks back to back, and the music and atmosphere make the game even more solid. The more anime approach to this makes me love it even more as well, following the cliche (and likable) tropes of over the top moves. As for the combat itself, it's one of those games where combat is somewhat difficult to master properly, as reading your opponents cues is very important here, but pulling off combos feels very rewarding.

The characters are also very likable, all of them being voiced (very well) as well. Then there is the story. From the get go, it's already pretty clear something is fully off, but of course, you don't know what. The twists that the story brings make a simple “beat every boss up” kind of story to something that's genuinely interesting.

While the game isn't too long (about 8 hours or so), it feels like the right length for a game like this. Especially since you can keep track of scores as well, so aiming to get better is always a thing you can do. All in all, it's absolutely worth a shot and a gem of a game if you are into hack-and-slash type of games!
