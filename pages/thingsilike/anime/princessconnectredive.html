[ Return to anime I like ]

“You better win Pecorine! Win and come back to us!”

Originally, I started watching it as a joke or “I'm bored, I need something stupid to watch.” or something. However, I ended up just watching all currently available episodes back then in one sitting and excitedly waiting for the next episode. What started as a joke, ended up with something I've grown really fond of somehow.

The story looks light-hearted, but there's a surprisingly dark side to it, something I'm not going to spoil because you really have to see that all for yourself. Though, most of the episodes are the main cast (the Gourmet Guild) visiting and meeting other characters of the series, which is generally light-hearted, save from a few episodes. Most of the darker stuff gradually builds up for the climax at the end. As for the Gourmet Guild themselves, as you will see them the most, it's not exactly a secret how fond I am of Pecorine. She is bubbly, energetic, and somewhat ditzy (and eats way too much for her own good, but that aside.) However, the rest of the guild, being the tsundere Kyaru, the caring Kokkoro, and the (literally) quiet yet heroic Yuuki are just as great.

And then there are the visuals. I was surprised with how good most of the more action packed scenes looked (one of them I have shown off with a screenshot underneath this.) It's so vibrant and colorful, but yet isn't distracting or painful to look at. But even outside of the action scenes, the vibrancy of the characters and the places are very pleasant to look at, though it does shine the most during the action. As for the music, it's really nice, and some of them really stand out, like this one, where the music alone just gets me excited, even without seeing the scenes playing. Which then always wants me to see specific scenes just to feel the goosebumps.

It's very safe to say that this anime made quite an impact on me. I will admit it's nothing special per se, so don't expect something revolutionary or amazing. However, it has been a surprise hit for me, and perhaps it will be for you as well if you're gonna watch it!
