[ Return to anime I like ]

“Fun things are fun!”

While I am not exactly sure what the reason was when I originally watched this, as nobody really recommended it to me, I do not regret it in the slightest. To this date, I still hold this anime dear, which shows, considering it is the first (and only) manga series I own, as well as my first anime figure one being of Yui Hirasawa.

Story wise, there isn't much going on. After all, it is a slice of life anime about girls doing cute things while in a music club living their life. That being said though, that makes the characters stand out even more. I am particularly fond of Yui and Ritsu myself, and those two together are also just fantastic together, with both having these moodmaker-like personalities. Though, Yui is quite a bit more airheaded, with one scene of her getting distracted by a dog meters away from where she is supposed to meet her friends. Unfortunately though, the pacing of the first season is quite poor, with a lot of time passing in too few episodes, something that is not at all happening in the second season, having a much better pacing.

It's pretty obvious at first glance that this is the work of KyoAni, as they used this pleasant visual and character style a lot back in the day. It's cute, it's appealing, and it works well for this. As for the music, considering the main theme is music, it's really good! Back in the day I used to listen to it a lot more than now, but some of the tracks are still in my main vocal playlist. Even if you aren't really into all of this, tracks like “U & I” (which is my favorite) and “My love is a stapler” are great songs to listen to (if you are into this kind of music of course.) There are even a bunch of tracks that are not in the anime, mainly the character songs.

This is one of those anime where you just sit down and watch while being all comfy. It's the cast that will absolutely give you a smile every time there is some funny interaction between the characters. And even if you don't like the anime, it never hurts to give the music a try!
