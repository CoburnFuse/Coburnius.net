[ Return to games I like ]

“Let me be your eyes! We'll do this together!”

When the initial trailer dropped for the DLC, I got incredibly excited. While the DLC didn't show much at all, aside from a few screens of battles, and it being about Honest, someone you meet in the main game, I did watch it countless times because I wanted to play it so badly.

The story here takes place more or less after the main game. This time the story is centered about Honest and her wanting to escape the place she got trapped in. You will meet some old characters but also some new ones. There are also a bunch of new bosses (some of them shown in the trailer) like a cat or a pirate captain. These bosses do feel a lot better than the bosses in the main game, so it's pretty clear they have learned a lot. And as weird as these bosses may sound, they do fit very well in the story. During the time together with Honest, you will learn all about a company that made programs, as well as the struggles they have been having.

The gameplay is more or less the same. It's still snake mixed with bullet hell, though there are some minigames in-between when hacking doors. Graphically, it's very consistent with the main game, and that makes me glad considering the style is something I always liked. The music is also something that I am very happy with and is as fantastic as the main game.

If you liked the main game, you should really just purchase the DLC when you are done with the main game. Just keep in mind that the game can still be as frustrating considering its difficulty!
