[ Return to games I like ]

“We're gonna fight this thing and we're gonna do it TOGETHER!”

I had seen this game pass through my feed a few times, but wasn't exactly sure what to think of it back then. However, I completely changed my mind when one of the songs was in one of those “Guess the video game music” videos. I then decided to look it up, and give it a try. It ended up being one of my all-time favorite games.

So, while this is your typical creature collection game, it's the combat that is incredibly interesting to me. Normally, when you think of type advantages, you'd think it would change the damage output. That is (normally) not the case here, rather, you get buffed or de-buffed depending on your element and what element is hitting you. This on its own makes strategizing a lot more interesting, but of course that's not where it ends, as there are a lot of moves that grant effects, allowing you to build around that as well, leading to possible chaos each and every time. Then there is also the entire fusion system, allowing you to fuse with your partner, granting some effects, stat boosts, and more strategy. Another small feature, but a fantastic one at that, is the possibility to change some difficulty settings regarding enemies. You can make it easier or harder, depending on what you want.

Story wise, it's surprisingly deep for something as a creature collection game. You got a main story, some big side stories, and some smaller quests (often related to the rest of the story.) Each character has their own story, detailing their character quite well. There are some characters I like more than others, but when it comes to the partners, I can't really think of one that I really dislike. Even the big bosses (called arch-angels) are likeable in their own way because they are so bizarre and unique, of course with me liking some more than others.

Graphically, it's very cute pixel-art. Everything feels consistent (unless purposefully out of place), and it's also really vibrant. The designs are cute, cool, or creepy, or whatever, depending on the monster. However, what I really want to talk about is how amazing the music is. The overworld music is pretty relaxing overall, but it's the battle music that stands out so much. Every battle theme has two versions, an instrumental one, and a vocal one. It is so incredibly difficult to not sing (read “scream”) along whenever you fuse, and the vocal music starts to play. A few notable examples that you find early on are “Like Chimeras” and “Same Old Story”. Of course there are more great songs, but you really have to find those yourself as I don't want to spoil even more than I already have.

Honestly, I can't say much more before it does actually become actual excited incoherent rambling. It is one of the few games I very happily grinded through to get all achievements, and I will happily do it again. I've got merch of this game left and right, which is only fair for one of my favorite games!
